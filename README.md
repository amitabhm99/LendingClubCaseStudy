## Conclusions
- Applicants taking loan for 'home improvement' and have income of 60k -70k have higher changes of defaulting
- Applicants whose home ownership is 'MORTGAGE and have income of 60-70k have higher changes of defaulting
- Applicants who receive interest at the rate of 21-24% and have an income of 70k-80k have higher changes of defaulting
